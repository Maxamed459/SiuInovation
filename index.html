Who We are?

At the SIU-Innovation and Skill Center, we promote professionalism, innovation, and entrepreneurship. We empower young entrepreneurs and professionals in Somalia to explore their creativity in a supportive environment. Our team guides startups from their early stages, inspiring impactful solutions that drive progress globally.
